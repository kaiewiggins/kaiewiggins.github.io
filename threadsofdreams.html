Threads of Dreams


When the seas rose, the monsters rose with them . . .

Creatures of folklore and myth collide with the climate apocalypse in a genre-bending YA fantasy-meets-ecopunk-meets-horror saga of monsters and magic.

Recommended for ages 14 and up.
