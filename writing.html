Writing, Rewriting & Editing Resources

  • On Writing by Stephen King - I found this book hugely encouraging; great to read at any stage in your writing process.

  • Techniques of the Selling Writer by Dwight V. Swain - if you read nothing else on the subject, read this book. Insightful, a little over the top, but beautifully encapsulates the things that matter when it comes time to plot/rewrite/edit.

  • The Elements Of Style (2011 Edt.) by William Strunk - a classic, required reading and useful reference guide. Quick and cheap. The new edition is marginally less irritating than the original (sorry).

  • Self-Editing for Fiction Writers by Renni Browne - you almost certainly need this book. Unless you just want to pay money to other people to do the work for you. That's ok too (pick me!)
