Blind the Eyes

When hope kills and dreams are deadly, your only chance for survival is to obey.

Cole reaches out to a corpse, indulging her forbidden fascination with the dead, and witnesses the dream that killed it. Her momentary disobedience sets off a chain of discoveries that tear her world apart and send her on a quest to expose the Tower's lies and stop the dying.


A story about identity, trauma & taking back your choices.

YA paranormal dystopian fantasy. 80,000 words. Coming Summer 2017.

Flame of the Connarii


Edana, eldest daughter of the Connarii chief, is fighting to lead her tribe through the treacherous Otherworld mists to a safe haven.

Torchann is the beast-raised last son of an extinguished race, master of a dying world.

Their worlds are destroyed, their very way of life, threatened. Can they unite, fight back and survive, or will they each die alone?


A story about home, belonging & leading well.

YA Celtic-flavoured Fantasy adventure. Serial webfiction updated Tuesdays.

Things Got Out of Hand


She almost died. Then he showed up.


April prefers her own company, but when Henry saves her from a homicidal Nightmare and moves in, her days of peaceful solitude are over.


A story about monsters, heroes, and plans that change.

Rom-com retelling of Blind the Eyes. Serial webfiction updated Fridays.

About

YA novels by K.A. Wiggins and webfiction serials by Kaie featuring difficult female protagonists overcoming barriers and fighting for a better world.

Expect various flavours of fantasy; future dystopian, contemporary dark, supernatural/paranormal and Celtic-inspired Otherworld.

In 2015 I quit my day job to gamble on making a go of it writing full time. Now I write stories and freelance while wandering Scotland, Cornwall & the Pacific Northwest.
