K.A. Wiggins (Kaie) is an award-winning Canadian speculative fiction author, speaker, and creative educator known for the celebrated ecopunk/Climate Fiction meets gothic-dystopian YA Dark Fantasy series Threads of Dreams.

She writes across fantasy, science fiction, and horror subgenres (often within the same work) for readers of all ages, exploring the tangled webs of society, environment, and identity through intricate, dreamlike tales of monsters and magic.

She has been published by Lightspeed Magazine, Strange Horizons, Pulphouse Fiction Magazine, Fantasy Magazine, Mysterion, The NoSleep Podcast, Renaissance Press, Hungry Shadow Press, and Virgibooks (in translation), among others, and is the president of the Children's Writers & Illustrators of British Columbia Society (CWILL BC), a member of The Writers' Union of Canada (+CSFFA & CVWS), and teaches internationally with the Creative Writing for Children Society (CWC) from her home in the Comox Valley, BC.
