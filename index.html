About

K.A. Wiggins writes monsters-and-magic-infused YA Fantasy with an edge for ages 14 and up. Expect more blood than kissing.

Her debut novel, BLIND THE EYES, is book one of the THREADS OF DREAMS trilogy. A not-quite-alive girl and her not-quite-dead ghost discover trusted authorities lie, allies have their own agendas and even the monsters wear masks. Join the newsletter to get an exclusive free 5 chapter preview ebook and audiobook.

A Vancouverite who never learned how to live in the same place for long, a bookstagrammer and a fan of Islay Scotch and craft gin, she tends to write challenging stories about prickly, unlikable people making trouble at the edges of things.

Kaie has been creating urban legends about witches in the back wood and fairies in the snow melt since she was seven years old. She's inspired by contemporary YA stars including Brenna Yovanoff, Kendare Blake, Melissa Marr, Kelley Armstrong, and Holly Black, and fantasy classic masters such as J.R.R. Tolkien, C.S. Lewis, and Madeleine L'Engle.

She currently works as a technical writer, copywriter, and marketing content creator while wandering Scotland, Cornwall & the Pacific Northwest.
