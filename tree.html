K.A. Wiggins

Author | Speaker | Creative Educator

K.A. Wiggins (Kaie) is an award-winning Canadian speculative fiction author, speaker, and creative educator known for the celebrated gothic-dystopian YA+ Dark Fantasy series Threads of Dreams.

Find her work in Strange Horizons, Lightspeed, Factor Four, Fantasy Magazine, Pulphouse, The NoSleep Podcast, Year's Best Canadian Fantasy & Science Fiction, and Brave New Weird: The Best New Weird Horror, among others.

She also serves as president of the Children's Writers & Illustrators of British Columbia Society (CWILL BC) and teaches with the Creative Writing for Children Society (CWC).
