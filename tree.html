K.A. Wiggins

Author | Speaker | Creative Services

K.A. Wiggins (Kaie) is an award-winning Canadian speculative fiction author, speaker, and creative writing/storytelling/publishing coach known for the celebrated gothic-dystopian YA Dark Fantasy series Threads of Dreams.

Find her work in Lightspeed, Fantasy Magazine, Pulphouse, The NoSleep Podcast, Adi Magazine, Mysterion, The Fairytale Magazine, and other leading genre and literary publications.

She also serves as president of the Children's Writers & Illustrators of British Columbia Society (CWILL BC) and teaches with the Creative Writing for Children Society (CWC).
